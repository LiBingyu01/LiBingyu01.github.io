Short Bio

I am a PhD student working on remote sensing vision, open-vocabulary segmentation, and video temporal reasoning. My work focuses on building robust visual-language and segmentation systems that generalize to diverse geospatial scenes and temporal sequences. I develop datasets, algorithms, and training strategies to bridge foundation models with remote sensing applications.
