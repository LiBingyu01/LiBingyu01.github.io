Short Bio

I am a PhD student focusing on applying multimodal large language models and vision-language models to visual tasks. I work on diverse scenes including natural, remote sensing, and underwater environments. My research includes dataset construction, algorithm development, and training pipeline design.

Advisors: 李学龙, 高君宇 · Collaborator: 张达
